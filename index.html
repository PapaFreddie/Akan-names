Akan Names Web Application

Akan names come from a traditional naming system for people from Ghana, whereby a child's name is given
according to the day they were born irrespective of their gender. This web application therefore, is developed to take a user's
birthday details and calculates the day of the week they were born and
then depending on their gender, it outputs their Akan Name.
