Akan Names Web Application

Akan names come from a traditional naming system for people from Ghana, whereby a child's name is determined by factors surrounding his or her birth. For example, the day of their birth or the manner in which they were born may determine which name they get irrespective of their gender. This web application therefore, is developed to take a user's birthday details and calculates the day of the week they were born and then depending on their gender outputs their Akan Name.

Akan Names and their Meanings

Female

  • Sunday: Akosua
  • Monday: Adwoa
  • Tueday: Abenaa
  • Wednesday: Akua
  • Thursday: Yaa
  • Friday: Afua
  • Saturday: Ama

Male

  • Sunday: Kwasi
  • Monday: Kwadwo
  • Tueday: Kwabena
  • Wednesday: Kwaku
  • Thursday: Yaw
  • Friday: Kofi
  • Saturday: Kwame
