Akan Names Web Application

Akan names come from a traditional naming system for people from Ghana, whereby a child's name is given according to the day they were born irrespective of their gender. This web application therefore, is developed to take a user's birthday details and calculates the day of the week they were born and then depending on their gender outputs their Akan Name.

Akan Names and their Meanings

Female

  • Sunday: Akosua
  • Monday: Adwoa
  • Tueday: Abenaa
  • Wednesday: Akua
  • Thursday: Yaa
  • Friday: Afua
  • Saturday: Ama

Male

  • Sunday: Kwasi
  • Monday: Kwadwo
  • Tueday: Kwabena
  • Wednesday: Kwaku
  • Thursday: Yaw
  • Friday: Kofi
  • Saturday: Kwame
