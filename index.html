Akan Names Web Application.

Akan names come from a traditional naming system for people from Ghana, whereby a child's name is given according to the day they were born irrespective of their gender.

This application takes a user's birthday details and calculates the day of the week they were born and then depending on their gender, it outputs their Akan Name.

Akan Names and their Meanings.

Males

  • Sunday:KWasi
  • Monday:KWadwo
  • Tuesday:KWabena
  • Wednesday:KWaku
  • Thirsday:Yaw
  • Friday:Kofi
  • Saturday:KWame

Females

  • Sunday:Akosua
  • Monday:Adwoa
  • Tuesday:Abenaa
  • Wednesday:Akua
  • Thursday:Yaa
  • Friday:Afua
  • Saturday:Ama
